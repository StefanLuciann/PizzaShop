It's-a-me Mario!

At It's-a-me Mario , we believe that pizza is more than just food – it’s an experience. Founded in 2020, our mission has always been to bring joy to our customers through authentic pizza, crafted with the finest ingredients and a true passion for flavor.

We use only fresh ingredients, homemade dough, and traditional recipes for our sauces. Our chefs put their heart into every pizza – whether it’s a classic Margherita or a fully customized creation.

Over the years, we’ve served thousands of happy customers and we are proud to be known as “the best pizzeria in town.”

Our Mission

To bring the authentic taste of Italian pizza to every neighborhood, offering speed, quality, and a memorable dining experience.
